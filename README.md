## Overview

This project presents a **Furniture Sales Dashboard** developed using Microsoft Power BI.  
The dashboard provides an interactive analysis of sales performance across the United States, allowing users to explore trends, shipping performance, and regional sales distribution.

The goal of this project is to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights that can support **data-driven decision-making** for sales strategy, logistics planning, and operational performance.

The dashboard highlights key metrics such as total sales, profit, quantity sold, geographic distribution, top-performing cities, shipping methods, and delivery durations.

# Business Objective

The primary objective of this dashboard is to:

• Analyze overall sales performance across the United States  
• Identify top-performing locations and cities  
• Understand shipping patterns and delivery performance  
• Monitor profit and sales trends over time  
• Support decision-making through visual analytics  

This dashboard allows stakeholders to quickly identify trends and performance indicators without manually reviewing large datasets.

# Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard highlights three primary KPIs that summarize overall business performance.

### Total Sales
**$391K**

Represents the total revenue generated from furniture sales across all locations.

Year-over-year performance shows a **9.82% increase**, indicating strong revenue growth.

---

### Total Profit
**$7K**

Shows the net profit generated from furniture sales.

The dashboard indicates a **55% decrease in profit year-over-year**, which may suggest increased operational costs, discounts, or pricing adjustments.

---

### Total Quantity Sold
**4.2K Units**

Represents the total number of furniture items sold during the reporting period.

The quantity metric increased by **6.74% year-over-year**, showing growth in customer demand.
